The Last Champions League, League Phase

The End of the League Phase

Today is the last day of the league phase before the playoffs and the knockout stages. Two teams have already been confirmed in the knockout stage: Arsenal and Bayern Munich. The rest of the teams can still lose their place in the top 8 to the playoff stage.

As the final matches is coming to an end, goal difference and head-to-head results will decides, who advances directly and who must play in the playoffs to advance in the round of 16. Every goal matters in this last round.
